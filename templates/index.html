About this tool: This model predicts whether an elderly individual (aged 60+) is likely to have high healthcare demand based on demographic, clinical, and lifestyle factors. It was trained on NHANES survey data using a Logistic Regression classifier — the top-performing model among Logistic Regression, Decision Tree, and Random Forest.
